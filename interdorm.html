Interdorm (non-resident) Room Visiting

Interdorm visiting involves non-residents visiting individual resident rooms within a given dorm. Non-resident visitation within personal rooms will commence after each dorm has had the opportunity to set community expectations and norms, for upper level students that is typically after Outings Week and for lower school students it is after Fall Family Weekend.
